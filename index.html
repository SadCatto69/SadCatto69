About Us

WE ARE THE BEST

Achbel Batna, was established in 2001 is a training center for the following sports: (Karate, Taekwondo, Kung Fu, Jiu Jitsu, Judo) at competitive prices.

It has several branches located in several regions of the state of Batna, one of its advantages is that it has highly experienced trainers, and it also meets all transportation needs and Places to shower.

Also, in Achbel Batna, we offer many high quality sports clothing and products at the best prices in the market.
